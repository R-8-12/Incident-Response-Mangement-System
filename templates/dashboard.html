FAQs

Regarding your queries

How can I report an incident?

After visiting, registering/logging-in you will be navigated to your dashboard. Here, on clicking 'Report an Incident', you'll be again navigated to incident report form. There you can report your incident with a suitable title and a description.

Is my reported incident information secure?

The incident report information like Incident-Title and its description, will be publiced as that must be visible to entire staff of your organisation. But except that none of your information like username and email will be shown publically.

Can I view the incidents reported by others?

Affcourse you can view the incidents reported by other users. For that you must be logged-in and will be navigated your dashboard. There you've to click on 'Response to an existing Incident'. There you can view every incidents by everyone within your organisation.

How quickly can I expect a response after reporting an incident?

Well there's a dedicated team to respond to your queries regarding the incidents. But everyone from your organisation is allowed to response as quicly as possible to your Incident. As well as you are too supposed to reply on incidents as fast as possible, if you've any idea regarding that.
